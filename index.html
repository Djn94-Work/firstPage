In the beginning

I was born in Houston, Texas and have lived on the south side my entire life. My younger days were filled with musical studies and performances that led me to most states in the country, and much of who I am today is owed to the fine educators and mentors I was lucky to be placed under the tutelage of. Originally, I began learning guitar as a child, but soon after picked up the clarinet for school, where I also taught myself tuba (the key to my touring the country) and switched permanently from guitar to bass, which is primarily what I perform on now-a-days around Houston (and sometimes San Antonio or San Angelo). I've experienced an amazing variety of ensemble to participate in, including but not limited to:

  • Bass trombone in University Jazz Band
  • principle tuba in Universty Orchestra/Wind Ensemble
  • principle bass clarinet in University band
  • I was drafted into SFA's choir during a conducting class
  • contrabass bugle in mulitiple world-renowned Drum and Bugle Corps
  • Electric and double bass in jazz ensembles and death metal bands
